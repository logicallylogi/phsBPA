About BPA

BPA is an organization that hosts competitions in a variety of fields, including but not limited to IT, Finances, Microsoft Office, Business Law, Marketing, and Management.

Plaques are given to the Top 3, and there is potential for scholarships or scholarship opportunities. You'll get to compete either with a team or alone in 2 categories and the top 10 proceed to the next leadership conference, eventually leading up to that year's best in the country.

"We could've gone to Florida if it wasn't for COVID. It's a great opportunity and I can't wait until next year" - William Kenzie (9th, BPA NLC Qualifier)
